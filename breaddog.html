Classic Bread Dog

What is better than a good ol' bread dog

Enjoy anywhere, anytime!

Ingredients

  • 1 slice of whole grain bread
  • Butter(optional)
  • 1 hotdog
  • Condiment or topping of choice(optional)

Instructions

  1. Take 1 slice of bread and cover the surface with butter
  2. Place hotdog in microwave or boiling water for 1-2 minutes
  3. Place the hotdog onto your bread slice
  4. Add your favorite sauces or toppings
  5. Enjoy your 3 minute meal!
