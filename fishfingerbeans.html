Fish Finger Baked Beans Special!

The kids loooove this one

Simple, quick and delightful

Ingredients

  • Fish fingers
  • Biscuits
  • Baked beans
  • Condiment or topping of choice(optional)

Instructions

  1. Wrap a baking tray in cooking foil and spray with non stick spray
  2. Butter could be used to cover the surface instead in step 1
  3. Bake in oven for 15-20 minutes until golden brown
  4. Remove and cool down for at least 7 minutes
  5. Place fingers, biscuits and baked beans onto a plate, don't be afraid to get creative
  6. Enjoy your fun and tasty meal
